Club Contributions


league contributions are done at the start of the season for ksh.100 each player to the club captain for.Registration fee for the club is ksh 1100 the rest is deposited to the clubs accounts

Referree Contributions


Referee contributions are done every weekend we are having a match at ksh 20 per player to cater for the referee expenses and the lines men too.

Cleaning Contributions

To clean our jerseys we contribute ksh 10 per player for the cleaning expenses

Training Contributions

Training Contributions are ksh 20 per player to cater for expenses that may arise e.g repairing the ball,purchasing a new ball

Joining Contributions

To join our team ur subject to ksh 100for the whole season

Medical Contributions

Medical contributions are contributed by each player at ksh 100 for medical expenses like medical bill for an injured player or for deep heat and bandages
